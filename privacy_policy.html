Privacy Policy for ChessUno

Last updated: 25 June 2026

ChessUno (“the app”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) is a mobile and web chess game operated by an independent developer, Ulug’bek Baxodirov. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using ChessUno, you agree to the practices described here.

If you have any questions, contact us at baxodirovulugbek593@gmail.com.

1. Who we are

ChessUno is a chess game with an optional card layer. It can be played:

  • Offline — local two-player (“hotseat”) and games against an on-device computer opponent. These modes require no account and send no data anywhere.
  • Online — two-player matches over the internet, with optional accounts, friends, and game history.

The online and account features are powered by Google Firebase (Authentication and Cloud Firestore). Google processes that data as our service provider; see Google’s Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

2. Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to provide accounts, online play, and friends.

Account information (only if you create an account or sign in):

  • If you sign up with email and password: your email address (used to authenticate you) and an encrypted password managed by Firebase Authentication. We never see or store your raw password.
  • If you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile photo as provided by Google Sign-In.
  • If you play as a guest: an anonymous account identifier only — no name, email, or personal details.

Profile and social data (for real, non-anonymous accounts):

  • A user ID, your chosen display name, your profile photo URL or a chosen in-app avatar, and an automatically generated friend code.
  • The list of achievements you have unlocked (which earned badges you hold).
  • Your friends list, friend requests, and game challenges.
  • Your online game history and saved game replays.
  • Your game statistics (e.g. win/loss counts and achievement progress). These counters are private to you — other players can see only which achievement badges you have earned, never the underlying numbers.
Note: your email address is not stored in your public profile. Other users can see only your display name, photo or avatar, friend code, and the achievement badges you have earned.

On-device data (stored only on your device, never uploaded):

  • Your achievement progress and statistics, your saved single-player game against the computer, whether you have seen the tutorial, your chosen language, and your chosen board/piece appearance. This information stays on your device, is removed when you uninstall the app, and is also cleared if you delete your account from within the app (see Section 6).

3. Information we do NOT collect

ChessUno does not:

  • show ads or use any advertising identifiers;
  • use third-party analytics or tracking;
  • collect your location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera;
  • sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

4. How we use your information

  • create and secure your account and sign you in;
  • enable online matches and synchronise game state between players;
  • provide friends, friend codes, challenges, achievements, and game history/replays;
  • display your name and photo or avatar to opponents and friends so they can recognise you.

5. How information is shared

  • With other players: when you play online, your display name and the moves of that game are visible to your opponent. Your friends and opponents can see your display name, profile photo or avatar, friend code, and the achievement badges you have earned. Your game history, replays, and statistics counts are private to you.
  • With Google Firebase: as our backend provider, to store and synchronise the data described above.
  • We do not share your information with any other third parties, except where required by law.

6. Data retention and deletion

We keep your account data for as long as your account exists.

  • You can delete your account at any time inside the app (Profile → Delete account). This permanently removes your profile, friend code, friends, online game history, saved replays, achievements and statistics, and deletes your authentication account. It also clears all related data stored on your device (achievements, saved single-player game, settings, and tutorial progress), returning the app to a fresh state. This action cannot be undone.
  • If you are unable to use the in-app option, you may request deletion by emailing baxodirovulugbek593@gmail.com, and we will delete your data within a reasonable time.

7. Children’s privacy

ChessUno is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

Account and game data are stored using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard security and encryption in transit. Access to your stored data is restricted by server-side security rules. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can exercise the deletion right directly in the app (see Section 6) or by contacting us at the email below.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
