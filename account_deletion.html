Delete Your ChessUno Account & Data

App: ChessUno (com.chessuno.app)
Last updated: 25 June 2026

ChessUno lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it.

Option 1 — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open ChessUno and sign in.
  2. Tap your avatar (top right) → Profile.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm (email/password accounts re‑enter their password for security).

Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email baxodirovulugbek593@gmail.com from the email address on your account with the subject “Delete my ChessUno account”. We will verify and delete your data, typically within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your public profile (display name, photo, friend code, chosen avatar);
  • Your friends, friend requests, and challenges;
  • Your online game history and saved replays;
  • Your achievements and statistics; and
  • Data stored on your device (achievements, saved offline game, settings).

What is kept

Nothing personal is retained after deletion. We keep no backups of deleted account data beyond standard short‑term system backups, which cycle out within 30 days.
